About SciDiscover

Democratizing scientific discovery by connecting the unconnected. We're building AI tools that help researchers uncover hidden relationships across neuroscience, AI, medicine, and physics.

Our Story

SciDiscover started as a hackathon project by four first-year engineering students at DY Patil University who saw a simple problem: researchers are drowning in papers and missing connections between fields. We built SciDiscover to help them see what they're missing.

In just 72 hours, we went from idea to working prototype. Today, SciDiscover helps researchers discover hidden connections between neuroscience and AI, protein folding and machine learning, quantum physics and biology.

We're not backed by big labs or corporations. Just four students who believe that the next big scientific breakthrough is hiding in the connections we haven't found yet.
